THE GOLDEN COLLECTION OF SQUID GAME EDITS

Dramatic and silly, fashionable and niche, long and short - they all survived the game. This time, not the Squid Game, but the one much worse - the game of choice and decency. These handpicked masterpieces have proved their worth and dignity to stand beside each other. They demonstrate the power, core values, and deep meaning of the fandom. It's been long time since they first appeared in my gallery, but now, I feel, is their time to come before the world. I can bet that no one of you will ever, ever see anything similar again. Many don't even realise what they've just encountered. But for those who do - enjoy watching.
